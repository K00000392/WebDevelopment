CECILY KENNY

ABOUT

Cecily Kenny was born in Limerick and graduated from Limerick School of Art and Design in 2004 with a BA in Fine Art Printmaking. Upon graduating she won the RDS Printmaker of the year award and was also awarded a yearlong bursury in the limerick Printmakers, culminating in her first solo show. She went on to complete her Masters in Multidiscilplinary Design in the University of Ulster, Belfast in 2013. Currently she is working towards gaining a Higher Diploma in Creative Mulitmedia Programming in Limerick Institute of Technology.
